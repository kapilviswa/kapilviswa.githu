About

My name is Kapil. I love to design, My field of interest is mainly in Apple platforms — iOS including iPhone and iPad, watchOS, macOS... I recently started to design for tvOS. I work in Android and Web platforms as well, mostly for work, not in my personal projects.

I have been doing design for 4 years; I am now working as a Product Designer in Ford. I am working on a Multi-Modal Journey Planning app for Ford Smart Mobility. The app is more like Transit but with payment option, first and last mile options as well as real-time tracking. I am the lead designer for iOS as well as Android.

I spent my spare time learning new things. I recently bought the 2018 iPad pro which has the face id. I installed procreate and started drawing creative things. I am learning Cinema4D as my interest in Augmented reality is increasing every day and wanted to explore the 3D options in UX. If you are interested in chatting, Email me and let’s get talking. ✌️
